Breakfast

  1. Smoothie Bowl: Blend fruits with almond milk, then top with nuts, seeds, and granola.
  2. Chia Seed Pudding: Combine chia seeds with almond milk and let them soak overnight; then add honey and fresh fruits.
  3. Omelet: Whisk eggs and fill with veggies like spinach and tomatoes, then top with cheese.

Lunch

  1. Quinoa Salad: Cook quinoa with tomatoes, cucumbers, feta cheese, and oil dressing.
  2. Lettuce Wraps: Fill large lettuce leaves with grilled chicken, avocado, and fresh salsa.
  3. Zucchini Noodles: Spiralize zucchini, sauté in olive oil with garlic, and add marinara sauce.

Dinner

  1. Grilled Salmon: Serve salmon fillets with roasted sweet potatoes and steamed broccoli.
  2. Zucchini Noodles: Sauté spiralized zucchini with marinara sauce and add meatballs.
  3. Curry: Create a chickpea or vegetable curry using coconut milk, served over rice.
