Using the infomration you provided us, we've compiled a list of meal ideas for breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Breakfast

  1. Smoothie Bowl: Blend your favorite fruits with almond milk and top with nuts, seeds, and gluten-free granola.
  2. Chia Seed Pudding: Mix chia seeds with almond milk and let it sit overnight. Add honey and fresh fruits in the morning.
  3. Omelet: Fill with veggies like spinach, tomatoes, and bell peppers, and sprinkle with cheese.
  4. Quinoa Porridge: Cook quinoa in almond milk and top with berries and a drizzle of maple syrup.
  5. Tofu Scramble: Sauté crumbled tofu with spinach, tomatoes, and spices.

Lunch

  1. Quinoa Salad: Toss cooked quinoa with cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, feta cheese, and a lemon-olive oil dressing.
  2. Lettuce Wraps: Fill large lettuce leaves with grilled chicken, avocado, and salsa.
  3. Zucchini Noodles: Sauté spiralized zucchini with olive oil, garlic, and your favorite marinara sauce
  4. Rice Bowl: Top brown rice with black beans, corn, avocado, and lime for a filling meal.
  5. Chickpea Salad: Combine chickpeas, diced cucumber, tomatoes, and tahini dressing.

Dinner

  1. Grilled Salmon: Serve with a side of roasted sweet potatoes and steamed broccoli.
  2. Zucchini Noodles: Toss spiralized zucchini with marinara sauce and meatballs (check sauce for gluten-free).
  3. Curry: Make a chickpea or vegetable curry using coconut milk and serve over rice.
  4. Taco Night: Use corn tortillas and fill them with seasoned meat, beans, cheese, and fresh toppings
  5. Stuffed Eggplant: Fill with spiced ground meat and rice.
